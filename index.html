Booster Gold

Booster Gold, also known as Michael Jon Carter, is a charismatic and sometimes controversial hero from the future who has captured the hearts of comic book fans since his debut in 1986. Armed with high-tech gadgets, a flashy costume, and a desire for fame and fortune, Booster initially traveled back in time from the 25th century to our present day with dreams of becoming a celebrity superhero. Despite his initial motivations, Booster has evolved into a complex character known for his wit, charm, and occasional moments of genuine heroism. Join us on a journey through time and explore the thrilling adventures of this time-traveling hero!
